This is the dashboard of your MyRoboCon Portal.

You can view important information such as your **Team's Contact Person**, **his/her email,** your **Laptop's Asset ID**, your **BrainBox ID** and your **BrainBox's Wi-Fi Password**.

You can also view **Assigned Resources** for all participants or infomration for your team.
